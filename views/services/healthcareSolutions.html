Healthcare Solutions


Medinetcorp is a premier provider of healthcare revenue cycle outsourcing services to medical billing and coding providers, payers, hospitals, managed care providers and individual physicians in the US.

The Revenue Cycle Management directly affects the cash flow for the healthcare providers. The long cycle of obtaining revenue from payers is one of the major causes of higher costs along with unpredictability of payments.

Medinetcorp's Revenue Cycle Management practice ensures minimizing errors and denials to facilitate quick turnaround times. The focus is on improved cash flow and decision making. Medinetcorp has the expertise and solutions that improve revenue cycle metrics. Improved metrics produce accelerated cash flow which allows providers to improve patient care.

Our RCM services range from Pre-admission and pre-registration processes that include insurance verification , Patient admission/registration and stay in the hospital , Clinical documentation , Charge capture , Medical coding (patient health information entry and management by certified coders using standard medical codes ) , Medical billing to Account receivables management , Constant follow up with the payers for unpaid or underpaid dues/accounts receivables , Account receivables Denial Management , Review of reasons for non- payment of dues and lastly recovering the unpaid dues.

Medinetcorp's value proposition:
  • Quicker Reimbursement Rate and Higher Profitabiligy
  • Reduced Age Receivables
  • Superior Application of Expertise and Resources
  • Greater Control Over Operating Costs
  • Seamless Claim Realization
  • Reduced Staffing Issues and Training Time
  • Directing Internal Staff to Core-activities
  • Quick Denial Tracking and Efficient Appeal and Dispute Resolution
  • Improved Patient and Physician satisfaction

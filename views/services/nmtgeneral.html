General Transcription


Medinetcorp is a leading provider of outsourced transcription and dictation services.. We offer a complete range of general transcription, dictation and typing services individually tailored to satisfy the requirements of each of our clients. Whether you are a student, business professional, social activist, research analyst, consultant, project cordinator or webmaster, we have ability to transcribe any kind of dictations and thus ensure accuracy and efficency with the transcription project.

General Transcription is mostly classified into four main specialties (Business Transcription, Legal Transcription, Academic Transcription and Media Transcription), it can include any field of transcription (besides medical) that requires either audio or video recordings to be converted into text.
